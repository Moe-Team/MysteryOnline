Mystery online is an IRC client that lets users chat as if they were in a visual novel. It also boasts several functions that help players engage in mystery solving that the community makes.

Community

Mystery Online has a thriving community, with a diverse cast of characters that love both mysteries and visual novels! Come and meet many of wonderful members and discover new and exciting things.
Meet new people and make friends

Mysteries

Our community loves mysteries and aims to make a sport out of it using the Mystery Online client. Next to the numerous amounts of self written mysteries and riddles by the community, we also host unique and entertaining events on the client from time to time!
